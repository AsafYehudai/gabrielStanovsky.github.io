I am a PhD student at Bar Ilan University's NLP lab. I conduct research in the field of computational linguistics, under the supervision of Prof. Ido Dagan.
Before that, I completed my Msc thesis (also in computational linguistics) at the Ben Gurion University , under the supervision of Prof. Michael Elhadad.
